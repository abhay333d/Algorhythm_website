Technical Council

ALGORHYTHM, the annual technical fest of VIT, is a celebration of excellence and community service by students. Over the last few years, it has been a success. The fest features various activities, including fun events, technical brainstorming, and euphoric presentations. From conducting competitive events, where students can showcase their talents to hosting exhibitions and dignitaries from various fields, ALGORHYTHM keeps getting better each year, We will continue to deliver an exhilarating event, this year!
